 What is JUPEBHub?
JUPEBHub is a comprehensive, free web-based learning platform designed specifically for students preparing for the Joint Universities Preliminary Examinations Board (JUPEB) examination in Nigeria — the smarter alternative to JAMB for direct university entry.
Every year, over 30,000 Nigerian students register for JUPEB. Most existing preparation resources either cost thousands of naira or offer poor quality with no real learning experience. JUPEBHub was built to change that — permanently and for free.

✨ Features
📚 Study Hub

Step-by-step interactive study notes for all 15+ JUPEB subjects
Covers all three departments: Science, Arts, and Commercial
Topic-by-topic breakdown with key formulas, concept explanations, and mnemonics
Curated YouTube video references for every topic

🎯 CBT Practice Mode

Full Computer-Based Test simulator — exactly like the real JUPEB exam
Customisable by subject, topic, difficulty level, year, and number of questions
Difficulty modes: Easy → Medium → Hard → Very Hard
Built-in countdown timer
Real past questions from 2015–2024 JUPEB exam papers

🤖 AI-Powered Explanations

After every question answered, an AI explains why the answer is correct — the what, why, and how
Powered by Claude AI (Anthropic)
Explanations written for Nigerian pre-university students in clear, simple language

💬 AI Study Chatbot

24/7 AI tutor that answers any JUPEB-related question
Context-aware — knows the student's department and subjects
Covers Science, Arts, and Commercial topics

📊 Progress Tracking

Personal dashboard with performance statistics per subject
CBT history with scores, correct/wrong breakdown, and date
Achievement badges and streak tracking
Personalised study recommendations based on performance

💡 Suggestions & Feedback
